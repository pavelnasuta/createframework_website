CREATE is not just a set of prompts, but a complete methodology that transforms chaotic ideas into profitable and scalable web services using artificial intelligence as the central tool. It solves the main problem when working with large language models: context loss.

Each subsequent prompt uses the results of the previous one as input data, so the AI model constantly "remembers" the entire accumulated context.

CONTEXT

Define the emotional core of the product and its fundamental idea. Form values that will guide AI in the development process and user interaction.

Vision & Mission

create, framework, AI

Formulate the fundamental problem you're solving and the transformation the user experiences. 

You are an experienced AI assistant specializing in product development. Your task is to help me formulate Vision & Mission for a new web service.

**Input data:**
1. **Raw idea:** I need to create a web service that will help people who have little time and a limited budget cook delicious and healthy food.

**Instructions:**
1. **Vision:** Formulate an inspiring vision that describes the ideal future we are creating. This should be a short and ambitious statement, for example: "Create a world where everyone has access to quality education."
2. **Mission:** Develop a clear and specific mission that explains what we do, for whom and for what purpose. The mission should reflect the fundamental problem we solve. Use the format "Our mission is [fundamental problem], turning [process] into [transformation]". For example: "Our mission is to democratize access to healthy nutrition, turning the routine of cooking into a creative and inspiring process."

**Expected result:**
Provide two clear, inspiring and concise statements: one for Vision and one for Mission, based on the provided idea.

Core Values

Vision & Mission from Prompt 1

Define key values that will become the 'ethical code' for AI directing its work and user interaction. 

You are an experienced AI assistant who now deeply understands our Vision & Mission. Your task is to help me define 3-5 key values (Core Values) that will become the "ethical code" for our product.

**Input data:**
1. **Vision & Mission:**
   - **Vision:** [Insert Vision from Prompt 1 result]
   - **Mission:** [Insert Mission from Prompt 1 result]

**Instructions:**
1. Analyze our Mission & Vision to understand what principles should guide our product.
2. Formulate 3-5 key values that will determine how AI generates content, interacts with users and makes decisions. For example, if one of the values is "simplicity," AI should not suggest complex recipes.
3. For each value, provide a brief description explaining what it means for our product and how it will influence AI behavior.

**Expected result:**
A list of 3-5 values with a brief explanation for each. For example:
**Simplicity:** AI will always offer simple and understandable solutions, avoiding complex recipes and instructions.
**Inclusivity:** The product will adapt to different needs, including diets, allergies and cooking skills.

The Emotional Problem

Vision from Prompt 1, Mission from Prompt 1, Core Values from Prompt 2

Focus on the emotion behind the task rather than the specific technical solution. 

You are an experienced AI assistant who is now fully immersed in the Vision, Mission and Core Values of our project. Your task is to help me identify the deep emotional problem (The Emotional Problem) that we solve.

**Input data:**
1. **Vision:** [Insert Vision from Prompt 1 result]
2. **Mission:** [Insert Mission from Prompt 1 result]  
3. **Core Values:** [Insert Core Values from Prompt 2 result]
4. **Raw idea:** Create a web service that will help people who have little time and a limited budget cook delicious and healthy food.

**Instructions:**
1. Analyze all provided data (Vision, Mission, Core Values) and the raw idea.
2. Ignore the literal task ("create a recipe app") and focus on the emotion behind this task.
3. Describe the emotional state of the user when they face the problem. For example, "the user doesn't just want to find a recipe, they feel overwhelmed and tired when standing in front of an empty refrigerator."
4. Describe how our product, guided by Mission and Values, will help transform this emotion, turning it into a positive experience.

**Expected result:**
A brief but comprehensive description of the emotional problem and how our product will solve it, allowing AI to create deeper and more personalized solutions.

Online Research & Trend Analysis

Vision, Mission, Core Values from stage C

Use AI to search and analyze current data from open sources: websites, articles, forums and social networks. 

You are an experienced AI assistant who deeply understands our Vision, Mission and Core Values. Now your task is to conduct internet research to identify key trends, common questions and user sentiments.

**Input data:**
1. **Product context:** [Insert Vision, Mission, Core Values from stage C]
2. **Raw data (examples):** [Upload keywords, topics, competitor names for search]

**Instructions:**
1. Analyze the provided data.
2. Use Google Search and other available tools to search for information on websites, articles, forums and social networks.
3. Identify and analyze key trends related to our idea.
4. Collect the most frequent user questions and their pain points from forums and review sites.
5. Assess the general sentiment in online discussions.

**Expected result:**
A structured report with conclusions on trends, a list of frequent questions and sentiment analysis.

Key Framework Values

Structural Approach

CREATE provides a clear step-by-step algorithm, eliminating chaos in the product development process and ensuring systematic progression from idea to launch.

AI as Core Tool

Unlike traditional methodologies, CREATE treats AI not as an auxiliary tool, but as the central element of the development process, maximizing its analytical and creative capabilities.

Context Preservation

Each stage builds upon previous results, ensuring AI maintains full context throughout the development cycle and makes increasingly informed decisions.

Practical Orientation

The framework focuses on creating real, profitable products rather than theoretical constructs, with each stage producing tangible deliverables.
